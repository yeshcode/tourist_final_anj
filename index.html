Island-Hopping in Mactan Island

A picturesque islets surround the waters of Cebu Island Province, and some are considered among the best in the Philippines.

Carnaza Island

The island features diverse natural landscapes including mangrove swamps, lagoons, hills, and forests, with white sand beaches, clear waters, hidden caves, and a scenic cliff offering panoramic views of the Visayan Sea.

See more

Sardine Run in Moalboal town

Moalboal offers a surreal underwater experience where you can swim with millions of sardines and spot turtles while snorkeling. This breathtaking spectacle is accessible year-round, even with just goggles, and guided tours are readily available in town.

See more

Hike to Osmeña Peak

Osmeña Peak, Cebu’s highest point at 1,013 meters, is part of the spire-studded Mantalongon Range in Dalaguete. Nicknamed the “Vegetable Basket of Cebu,” the town’s lush fields and livestock create a scenic path to the summit.

See more

Thresher Sharks

Malapascua Island is renowned for its vibrant marine life and world-famous dive spots. It’s the only place on Earth where divers can see thresher sharks daily, along with sightings of hammerhead sharks, manta rays, and blue ring octopuses.

See more

Water Adventures in Camotes Island

Camotes Island is a haven for adventure seekers. One of its top attractions is the Holy Crystal Cave, known for its downward-sloping chambers.

See more

Marine Biodiversity

Sumilon Island is one of Cebu’s most captivating spots, known for its shifting sandbar that changes with the tide. Surrounded by crystal-clear waters in stunning shades of blue, it offers an irresistible invitation to swim and explore.

See more

Beaches of Bantayan Island

One of the top activities in Cebu is visiting Bantayan Island, known for its pristine white sand beaches that rival Boracay’s beauty—all set in a peaceful fishing village atmosphere.

See more

Canyoneering to Kawasan Falls

Kawasan Falls, Cebu’s most famous attraction, is a stunning three-tiered waterfall with vibrant turquoise waters, offering one of the most unforgettable experiences in the Philippines.
